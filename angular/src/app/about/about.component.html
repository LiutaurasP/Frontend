Story
Philosophy: Ability to access complex analytics previously limited due to entry barrier is becoming easier as computational power is becoming more abbandant. Yet most of economic scenarios and insights are still generated by rather dated models while central bank models are too resource intensive for practical problems. Alpha huntsman project intends to provide access to complex analytics to formulate more informative decisions based on economic forces. Fundamentally, we believe that economic forces drive financial performance of all companies. Hence, to solve financial problems - we project them into our Atlas economic engine with numerous applications. For example, financial and economics news sources are relying on the old business model of selling data and one-off new articles. However, key themes are often revised with analytics present often discarded and modified with continiuity. Wouldn't it be great if we could translate fundamental economic developments into quantifable measures and apply that on our portfolio or private company analytics? That's the problem we are aiming to solve.
Atlas:
Our proprietary global economy engine coverng 41 countries simultanously and based of more than 35k variables. Contrary to popular approaches - all countries are modelled simultanously under assumption of high interconnectiveness.
Status:
In development
Beta engine:
Beta represents a measure of co-movement between variable of interest and underlying variate. Financial measures are decomposed to underlying key unobservable drivers. Performance is attributed to structural grouping: countries, sectors and industries.
Status:
Scheduled for 2020H1
Mu Engine:
Mu represents an average growth over time that is not explained by beta engine. Our view is that drift rate is driven by individual company performance. Individual performance is attributable by Atlas and forms the base of our strategic views.
Status:
Scheduled for 2020H2
Omega Engine:
Omega represents interconnectiveness of the set of assets. Ensuring efficient and robust select of weights allows to formulate superior portfolio decisions. Our view is that portfolio decisions should be formed by incorporating a wide range of what-if scenarios, similar how U.S. Federal Reserves evaluates policy appropriateness.
Status:
Scheduled for 2021H1
Alpha engine:
Alpha is the deviation of measure of interest that will move towards the fundamental level. Our views is that over medium horizon financial measures follow underlying economic forces. Individual equities move mainly during earnings releases but earnings is just a hidden variable driven by economic fundamentals. Over time and on average - fundamentals pervail. For example, tossing a fair coin and observing 100 heads in a row. Next toss is equiprobable but, unlike coins, socio-economic fundamentals tends to converge faster to conditional means.
Status:
Scheduled for 2021H2
